Wh- Questions
Wh- questions are questions that begin with words like who, what, where, when, why, or how. We use them to ask for information, not just yes or no.

Common Patterns for Wh- Questions
Pattern 1: Wh- word + do/does + subject + verb Example: Where do you live? Example: What does she eat?
Pattern 2: Wh- word + be (am/is/are) + subject Example: How are you? Example: Where is your book?
